Appearance


Relatively small in height and stature (duh, she's a kid), her tiny body doesn't seem malnourished but still is deceptively frail in appearance. With the skinniest arms and legs, she's certainly not an intimidating individual. Her eyes are wide and full of wonder, framed perfectly by a rounded face with squeezable cheeks and the cutest white-toothed smile. Her skin retains the same darker shade shared by the Kre'Nathians and local Tribals of the islands, and her eyes are a dark emerald green. The difference from the majority lies in her soft green hair, and it is indeed naturally colored that way, no dye involved. Usually braided, it's long enough to brush against the bottoms of her shoulder-blades.

Attire


Technically a princess from an ancient time, she most certainly dresses like one. The images provided are good examples of her usual attire, although if anything else is being worn it will be described in detail.

Personality


The child is a genuine and endearing soul. She is naive, innocent, easily trusting those around her unless they have done something for her to believe they are untrustworthy. Adventurous and brave, she can be found stubbornly diving head-first into danger. Her morals are strong, and she is very loud about them too, making her seem sometimes obnoxious but at least her heart's in the right place.
