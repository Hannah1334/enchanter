Observations

Appearance


Relatively small in height and stature (duh, she's a kid), her tiny body doesn't seem malnourished but still is deceptively frail in appearance. With the skinniest arms and legs, she's certainly not an intimidating individual. Her eyes are wide and full of wonder, framed perfectly by a rounded face with squeezable cheeks and the cutest white-toothed smile. While strange for the general area, her skin does is most certainly not pale, but retains the same darker shade shared by the Kre'Nathians and local Tribals of the islands, and her eyes are a dark emerald green. The difference from the majority lies in her soft green hair, and it is indeed naturally colored that way, no dye involved. Usually braided, it's long enough to brush against the bottoms of her shoulder-blades.

Attire


Technically a princess from an ancient time, she most certainly dresses like one. The images provided are good examples of her usual attire, although if anything else is being worn it will be described in detail. (Hell, there will be times when even the pictured attire will be described in detail.)

Personality


The child is a genuine and endearing soul. She is naive, innocent, easily trusting those around her unless they have done something for her to believe they are untrustworthy. Adventurous and brave, she can be found stubbornly diving head-first into danger. Her morals are strong, and she is very loud about them too, making her seem sometimes obnoxious but at least her heart's in the right place.

Enchantress

Rank: True Practitioner


"The art of magical augmentation. Enchanters are able to infuse objects with their magic, granting the object a either a "Permanent" effect or boon, or a temporary ability which dissipates with time. Permanent enchantments must be recharged with the Enchanter's mana to keep these effects/boons active. As an enchanter increases in power, they not only gain acess to stronger enchantments, but are able to increase their durability and longevity. Only non-biological/sentient objects may be enchanted."
Universals

Mana Manipulation


"The most basic class of magic, involving the direct manipulation of Mana, is available to all mages. Raw Mana is a mundane source of magic with no special qualities, but offers some level of versatility. The amount of Mana a mage is capable of replenishing or expelling is dependent on their overall level. (Most spells taught in class are under this tree. Spells taught in class must be on your character sheet, and any alterations or upgrades to taught spells must be discussed with a professor.)"
